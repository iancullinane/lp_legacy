Our Promise:

To deliver top quality janitorial services to commercial business and office cleaning through honesty, integrity, hard work and reliability.

We use a sophisticated management system that allows us to manage our cleaning technitians clock ins and outs through phone calls and fingerprint punch.

Alerts are sent to supervisors and management just 5 minutes past the technitian scheduled time in case of a missing clock in. This system allows us to know exactly the time our technitians spend on each cleaning which ensures quality control.

Each account has an assigned supervisor, the supervisor is required to have scheduled onsite monthly inspections and walkthroughs for quality assurance.
